BOOK

A Woman’s Worth

A woman's worth is a transformational book written to help women realize, develop and utilize the great potential God put in them to enhance their living standard and impact their world. Alot of women have gone to their graves without living the glorious life they were destined for due to ignorance. This book is a call for women to be deliberate and intentional in creating the kind of life they want and not going through life hoping and depending on handouts from those looking to exploit them. It challenges women not to accept the ancient stereotype and script written for them by their fellow humans that reduce them to baby making factories and objects for pleasure.....
